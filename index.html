Born for Innovation

I’m Chante’ Johnson, a technologist and learning experience designer with a passion for clarity, inclusion, and lifelong learning. Originally from Harlem, New York, I’ve lived in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Maryland, and now call Texas home. Every move, every challenge has been a lesson—and I carry that mindset into everything I create.

My name, given to me by my aunt, is French for “to sing”—and I’ve always believed in the power of voice, whether through storytelling, design, or digital strategy. I love reading, traveling, and gaining new skills, and I see every challenge as an opportunity to grow.

Academically, my path reflects the same layered approach I bring to my work:

  • My Master of Arts in Learning, Design, and Technology focuses on curriculum development, instructional strategy, and designing inclusive learning environments.
  • My Bachelor of Science in Computer Science blends business principles with front-end development, giving me the tools to build scalable, user-centered digital solutions.
  • My Associate of Science in Computer Information Systems laid the technical foundation—covering hardware, boot menus, Active Directory, networking, and system architecture.

    • Professionally, I’ve spent over 13 years creating learner-centered solutions across public service, government, healthcare, HVAC, internet, and cable industries. I specialize in bridging technical systems with human-centered design—developing accessible modules, responsive training sites, and content that empowers people to learn with confidence.

    I stay current with emerging technologies like AI by attending workshops, following industry podcasts, and engaging with thought leaders. I built this portfolio site from scratch to showcase not just what I do, but who I am: someone committed to innovation, equity, and using technology to make a meaningful impact.
